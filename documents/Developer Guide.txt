The main models of the project:

    Enumerations:
        The enums used in the project:
            a. Product -> Stores all the products that the user can subscribe to.
            b. City -> Store all the city supported
            c. Country -> Store all the country supported
            d. State -> Store the states
            e. Tax -> Stores the tax payment number.

1. Address: It will store the complete address of the user.

2. BusinessProfile: This will store the complete business information that will be common across all the products subscribed.

3. Product Subscription: This will store all the products subscribed.
